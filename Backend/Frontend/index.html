Mpesa service-fee based loan processing for Kenya

Apply in minutes using your phone number and National ID. Pay the required service fee to begin processing, then track your loan status on your dashboard.

How it works

  1. Create an account with your phone number, National ID, and password.
  2. Request a loan amount and confirm your M-Pesa number.
  3. Pay the service fee via Paystack (card / mobile money options supported by Paystack).
  4. We verify payment server-side and, if approved, disburse via Paystack then Transfered to your M-Pesa.

Important: By proceeding, you agree to provide accurate information. Keep your credentials secure. PesaFix will never ask for your password via phone call or SMS.
